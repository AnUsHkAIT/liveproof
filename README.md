# LiveProof

AI-powered resume matcher. Upload your resume, paste any job description, 
get your match score instantly. Free, no signup needed.

## Live Site
https://liveproof.onrender.com

## What it does
- Upload resume (PDF, DOCX, TXT) or type your skills
- Paste any job description from LinkedIn, Indeed, anywhere
- AI scores your match percentage instantly
- Shows exactly what skills you have and what you're missing
- Downloads a custom tailored resume PDF

## Company URLs
Send /for/google, /for/amazon, /for/meta to customize for specific companies.
